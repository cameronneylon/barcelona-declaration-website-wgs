Bologna Meeting on Open Research Information - Programme


The Bologna Meeting on Open Research Information took place on May 28th, 2025 at the University of Bologna, Bologna, Italy in the context of the 5th Workshop on Open Citations and Open Scholarly Metadata (WOOC2025).


Programme

Bologna Meeting on Open Research Information

9:30 Registration
10:00 Official welcome by Unibo - Raffaella Campaner, Vice Rector for International Relations
Welcome, housekeeping
10:15 Barcelona Declaration: Introduction & Where are we now
10:45 Short presentations by signatories
Carla Carbonell Cortés, Fundación “la Caixa”
John Chodacki, California Digital Library
Ana Ranitovic, Dutch signatories of the Barcelona Declaration
11:15 Coffee break
11:45 Introduction working groups & break-out sessions

Working group 3 - Funding metadata

16:00Coffee break
